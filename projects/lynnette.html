Lynnette Gamification

Project Description

Lynnette is an interactive software tutor developed under Dr. Vincent Aleven's research team at CMU HCII. Lynnette aims to teach middle school students concepts of algebra through practice problems in a game-like context. Lynnette is accessible by a drag and drop interface as well as a type-in interface.

My Role

My role was designing and implementing a type-in interface with game-like components. I developed lo-fi and hi-fi prototypes, generated interface graphics, and implemented animations.
