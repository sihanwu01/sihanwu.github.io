ScienceQuiz

This project is a mobile application development project from the course 67-272 at Carnegie Mellon University.

ScienceQuiz is a non-profit organization that aims to promote science by encouraging students to participate in a series of science quiz events. Students will register via organizations (typically schools) and form teams to compete by answering science-related questions and scoring points for their teams.

The purpose of science quizzing is to motivate students to learn more about science by having them engage in friendly competitions where they can demonstrate their knowledge by answering science-related questions.

The ScienceQuiz website back end was developed with Ruby on Rails, and the front end was developed with the help of Materialize.css.
