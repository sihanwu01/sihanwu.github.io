River Otter Virtual Tour

This project is a semester long project from the course 05-292 Learning in Museums

Virtual tours are live online sessions that allow students, families and visitors to engage with CMNHâ€™s different exhibitions remotely. For our project, we created a series of online activities to enhance the live virtual tour experience for elementary school students. We chose to focus on the story of the river otter in Western Pennsylvania to further tie in the Anthropocene lens for visiting students.

The activity plan is divided into three parts, as pre-tour, during, and post-tour activities. The overall activity plan is structured in a way that enables students to exercise what they have learned through reflections and active engagement. Students begin by briefly learning about the river otter story before the tour, engaging in a live activity during the tour, and reflecting on what they learned after the tour.
